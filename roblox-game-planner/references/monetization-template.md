## 3. First Purchase Bonus
- First time buying any product: 2x value
- Creates incentive to make first purchase

## 4. Robux-to-Currency Ratio
- What's 1 Robux worth in game currency?
- Does this make earning feel too slow for free players?

## 5. Limited-Time Offers
- Launch bundle: exclusive pet + coins at discount
- Weekly rotating offers
- Seasonal exclusives (Halloween pet, Christmas skin)

## 6. Ethics
- Can free players access ALL gameplay content?
- Is paying cosmetic-only or power too?
- What's the maximum advantage a payer has?

## 7. Revenue Estimates
- Expected conversion rate: 1-5% of players
- Price anchoring: cheapest item accessible, expensive items aspirational
